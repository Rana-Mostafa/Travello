Egypt officially "the Arab Republic of Egypt", is located mostly in the northern part of the African Continent with part of its Sinai desert located in the Asian Continent. It has a total area of 1,002,450 square km making it the 31st largest country in the world, measuring from the very North to the very south you have a total of 1,024 km., Cairo is the country's capital and largest city.

Egypt is very famous for its ancient civilization and the monuments of the majestic pharaohs, such as the Great Pyramids of Giza, the Great Sphinx, the Egyptian Museum, the GEM (The Grand Egyptian Museum), Sakkara, and Dahshur. In the south, there are famous monuments such as the Luxor Temple, the Karnak Temple, Abu Simbel, the Temple of Hatshepsut, the Valley of the Kings with emphasis on the tomb of Tutankhamun, the Temple of Edfu (Horus), the High Dam of Aswan, and the Philae Temple, The summer vacation cities like sharm El-shikh, Dahab, Hurghada and others. Popular landmarks







Dahab

Dahab is located on the Gulf of Aqaba, divided into two sections: the northern part of the town is the commercial district which includes markets, bazaars and shopping areas, while the southern part is called Al-Asala which is characterized by simple Bedouin life. Diving and Snorkeling is one of the main activities(The Blue Hole)and The Laguna Beach, Kitesurfing, Camping, Hiking.

Hurghada

Hurghada is the Pearl of the Red Sea, Activities like diving, snorkeling and deep sea fishing. the city is thriving with marine life, spectacular coral reefs and tiny colorful fish settlements that captured the world attention and became the Europeans obsession and their favorite escape destination in the winter with its white sand and clear turquoise water. Here are some of the plenty sites that you can visit both in water and on land in Hurghada city.

Marsa Alam

Marsa Alam is a town in South-Eastern Egypt, located on the western shore of the Red Sea. It is currently seeing fast increasing popularity as a tourist destination Due to its crystal clear water and its white sandy beaches, it is also known as the "Egyptian Maldives", A famous sea resort and a center for diving and sea trips. The shore contains amazing coral reefs and diving spots.Activities like Diving and amazing Nightlife.

Luxor

Luxor stands head and shoulders above Egypt's other towns for the sheer wealth of historic tourist attractions and things to do for travelers. The glut of Ancient Egypt's giant-sized temples and tombs lined with vibrant wall paintings you can visit here makes the town an open-air museum the great city of the Middle Kingdom and New Kingdom pharaohs, who covered the banks of the Nile with their mammoth building works and began the vast tomb structures snugly hidden amid the rocky valley of the West Bank.

Aswan

Aswan is a Nubian city in the south of Egypt located on the east bank of the Nile river. Aswan is the land of beauty and charming, the icon of the middle east, characterized by its the warm climate and rare plant islands, also there's rock Nile islands around the city extended from the high dam to the north of Edfu, There are archaeological areas like the crocodile island and elephantine island also famous with a Nile river cruise.

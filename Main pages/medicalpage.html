Turkey Medical Park Goztepe Hospital in Istanbul



Medical Park Hospital is one of the world's most renowned medical care providers. The medical group is highly experienced and understands the special needs and concerns of international patients.

The International Patient Centre of the hospital provides thorough assistance to international patients with all their concerns and queries before, during and after their stay.

The dedicated staff at the hospital is multilingual and multicultural, and offers a number of special services & amenities for overseas patients. The IPC team ensures that the patients are provided personalized services and best clinical care throughout their treatment.


Facilities for International patients:
  • Schedule appointments
  • Provide cost estimation and package prices for all treatments
  • Airport pick-up and drop services
  • Free of cost Medical Second Opinion
  • Free of charge translation services: in more than 20 languages. The interpreters are available in person, through telephone or any video services.
  • 24-hour emergency services, including air ambulance transfer coordination
  • Assistance with Visa invitation letters
  • Assistance with accommodation (hotel, guest houses etc.)
  • Coordination with primary insurance and travel assistance companies






USA The Johns Hopkins Hospital



Healthcare facilities in the United States are largely privately owned. Health insurance in the USA is primarily obtained through employers, independently through private purchase, or through government-based programs. Some low-cost and cost-free health care services exist through non-profit organizations, charities, and publicly funded programs.

However, international visitors should always carry insurance when they visit the United States. The cost of treating a serious emergency can be catastrophically high. The cost of getting medical services in the US is one of the highest worldwide, which makes sense to be insured when visiting the country.

Switzerland The Vaud University Hospital Center



The Vaud University Hospital Center (CHUV)provides care in all areas of medicine: from somatic diseases to psychiatric illnesses, medical and surgical disciplines, whether ambulatory or hospital.It includes 17 clinical, medico-technical, academic and administrative departments, as well as a psychogeriatric EMS in Gimel.

The CHUV provides treatment in all medical specialties, including psychiatry. It meets the needs of the population of the Lausanne agglomeration as a hospital of first resort. It is the reference hospital of the canton and a large part of French-speaking Switzerland, for patients who require specialized and ultra-specialized care.
